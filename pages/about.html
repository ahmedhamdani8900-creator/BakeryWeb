London Sweets & Bakers

Freshness, quality & tradition since 1988


London Bakers and Sweets is a premium bakery and sweets shop nestled in the heart of the city. We craft an exquisite selection of cakes, delicate pastries, crunchy cookies, savory bakery snacks, and time-honoured traditional sweets. Our foundation rests on three pillars: finest ingredients, impeccable hygiene, and genuine customer delight. From buttery croissants to rich plum cakes and aromatic cardamom biscuits – each creation tells a story of excellence.

Our Story

Passion for baking

It started in a small family kitchen with a wood-fired oven and a dream. Our founder, Mrs. Eleanor Baker, believed that baking is an act of love. Today, we continue her legacy by infusing warmth into every loaf, every pastry, and every sweet. We don’t just bake — we create edible joy.

Tradition meets innovation

We honour centuries-old British baking traditions while embracing modern techniques. Think vegan-friendly cupcakes, gluten-free fruitcakes, and naturally sweetened sweets — all without compromising taste. Our bakers artfully combine heritage recipes with contemporary flair.
